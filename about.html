About

The Intersection of Engineering, Product, and Art

My Story

I'm Sabaritha Sekar, and I work at the intersection of three interconnected identities that continuously inform and reinforce each other.

Engineer

My foundation is in deep systems thinking, advanced mathematics, robotics, and perception. I build robust technical foundations, solve complex problems, and create systems that are both elegant and reliable. This technical depth informs everything I do.

Builder/PM

Product intuition and customer empathy drive my work in product management and B2B solutions. I combine technical understanding with business acumen, turning ideas into impactful products that solve real problems. Systems thinking helps me see the bigger picture in product architecture.

Artist

Through ceramics, I explore the intersection of technical precision and creative expression. Glaze chemistry becomes a playground for material science, while craft teaches me about iteration, patience, and the beauty of imperfection. This artistic practice also becomes a vehicle for STEM mentorship, connecting hands-on learning with scientific principles.

The Connections

These identities don't exist in isolation. Engineering principles help me understand product architecture. Product thinking informs how I approach ceramic design. Artistic sensibility brings creativity to technical problem-solving. And teaching through craft reinforces my understanding of complex systems.

This portfolio is a reflection of how these three identities weave together, creating a unique perspective that spans technical depth, product intuition, and creative expression.
